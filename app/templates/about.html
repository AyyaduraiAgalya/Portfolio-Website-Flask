About Me

My philosophy is simple: technology should serve people. Whether it’s building resilient systems or deciphering complex datasets, I thrive on solving meaningful challenges that make a tangible impact. At the heart of my work is a dedication to collaboration, continuous learning, and creating solutions that resonate.

The collection included over 100 APIs, focusing on testing the logic of a web app used by doctors and a mobile app used by patients, achieving 99.9% test coverage for API scenarios.
  • Led the transition from a waterfall model to an agile development approach, introducing and optimizing tools like JIRA, Confluence, and BitBucket. Established sprint planning, backlog prioritization, and JIRA workflows, reducing costs by managing environments dynamically and streamlining processes.
